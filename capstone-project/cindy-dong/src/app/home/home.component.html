Overview:

About:

This project focuses on analyzing and visualizing data regarding uninsured individuals under the age of 65 across Virginia counties from 2009 to 2020. The objective of this tool is to provide policymakers and researchers with insights into the significant reduction in uninsured rates over time. It aims to enhance understanding of how policies and various factors have impacted health insurance coverage in these regions.

The data for this project is sourced from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Qualityâ€™s (AHRQ) Social Determinants of Health (SDOH) Database (Beta Version). Trends:

  • Year: This variable helps track changes and trends over time.
  • Total Weighted Population: This variable provides a measure of the total population in each county, accounting for factors such as age distribution.
  • Geography: This variable indicates whether a county is coastal or not, which can influence access to healthcare services.
